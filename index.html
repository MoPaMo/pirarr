pirarr is a Large Language Model (LLM) based on Google-T5, capable of translating English to Pirate Speak

trained on about 1k examples of English to Pirate Speak translations, including Mobi Dick and Treasure Island excerpts.

Try it out!

English

"Hello, how are you today?"

Pirate Speak

"Ahoy, how ye be farin' today, matey?"
